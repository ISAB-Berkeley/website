OFFICIAL ISAB STATEMENT

W

e are deeply saddened by the passing of one of our core members, Tarishi Jain. Tarishi had been a part of our family for two semesters, contributing both as an External Events Committee Member and subsequently, as External Events Committee Chair. Her passing has had a profound impact on our tight-knit community. Tarishi embodied our organization's values of diversity and guidance; she exuded kindness with her smile and consistently optimistic attitude; she displayed sincere dedication through her work, leaving a lasting impact on both ISAB and its members. In her time as External Events Chair, Tarishi initiated bold new projects, including "Color the Earth" to highlight the importance of protecting the environment. The event was a success and testament to her ability, perseverance, and innovation. Her unwavering enthusiasm for the organization and its members was undeniable. She was a leader members looked up to, but above all, she was a caring friend who we knew we could rely on. Our sincerest condolences to Tarishi's family and friends, as well as the grieving relatives of the other victims in this horrific event. Tarishi, you will always be a part of our ISAB family.

Aradhana Sachdev // International Student Association at Berkeley Fall 2016 President, on behalf of all members of ISAB
