Our Committees

ISAB Core consists of 8 committees, with each committee handling and organizing their own set of social or professional events. The committees are divided into three main sections: Internal, External and Operations. The External branch committees plan social and professional events, the internal division committees handle all the internal social

Associate of Operation

The Associate of Operations Committee is responsible in assisting with the operations of ISAB. From managing inventory, meeting minutes, assisting in finances, AO aims to ensure that the various other committees of ISAB can put forth their best work possible.

Collaborative Events Committee

The Collaborative Events Committee strives towards nourishing the ISAB General Membership Program. From organizing various outings to managing the family system, you will have the chance to turn your crazily fun ideas into reality. Whether you like writing or managing events, fostering diversity or forming close relationships, join and let your creativity shine!

External Events Committee

The External Events Committee creates and organizes ISAB events that aim to reach out to the wider campus community. By organizing events like the In-N-Out fundraiser, Global Games, Yacht Night and other large-scale events, we aim to connect with other clubs and organizations and effectively provide a platform for engagement with different cultures.

Historians

The Historian Committee is responsible for the photographic and videographic documentation of professional and social events. Through visual content, it aims to capture ISAB’s true spirit: the club’s formal aspects, the friendships, the multicultural community to which we are all proud to part of.

International Programs Committee

The committee's mission is to "act as a resource for international students to assist with any issues given their international student status in UC Berkeley, through events and programs in conjunction with BIO, ASUC and other organizations on campus." The International Program committee member's responsibilities will focus on assisting the Director in university relations.

Marketing Committee

The Marketing Committee is responsible for establishing ISAB’s presence on campus and for advertising the large events that ISAB hosts. The committee develops strategic, innovative, and creative marketing campaigns as well as graphics.


Social Committee

The Social Committee aims to cultivate and develop relationships between core members within ISAB. Whether through arranging frequent social events or organizing our semesterly retreat to bring ISAB closer together, this committee is comprised of people who have the affinity to bring people together in any situation. Responsibilities include planning retreats, daytrips, kickbacks, and an end-of-semester banquet.

Tech Committee

The Technology Committee is responsible for ISAB’s online presence. This includes the ISAB website and the creative & promotional media posted online. As a committee we work on improving and maintaining the website and build tools to aid in reaching out to alumni and to improve the communication within the club.
