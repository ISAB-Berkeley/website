Our Committees

ISAB Core consists of 7 committees, with each committee handling and organizing their own set of social or professional events. The committees are divided into three main sections: Internal, External and Operations.

The External division consists of three committees: External Events, International Programs and Marketing. These committees plan social and professional events and handle the outreach for the greater Berkeley community.

The Internal division consists of two committees: Social and Collaborative Events. These committees handle all the internal social events for the club, both for the core and general members.

The Operations division consists three committees: Historian, Associate of Operations and Technology. These committees are responsible for the logistics of the club such as inventory and finances, as well as documenting our events and keeping the website updated.

Social Media Committee

The Social Media Leads oversee and organize the content used on ISAB’s social media platforms. The committee focuses on advertising all ISAB events and providing insight on international student life at Berkeley. All the while, the Social Media Leads use all this content to engage with the international community at Berkeley and create connections across the world.

Social Committee

The Social Lead coordinates events that focus on maintaining a fun, social atmosphere of ISAB throughout the semester. The committee will organize activities that bring together both the core and general members and create a welcoming and family-like community. We will plan activities such as ISAB families, iDates, various socials whilst also brainstorming new ways to connect everyone.

Professional Committee

The Professional Lead organizes and provides international student oriented career and professional development opportunities for our members and the greater international community at Cal. The committee provides a great platform for international students to establish a network with alumni and professionals, as well as develop their own professional skills for recruitment / grad school and stay updated with employment policy guidelines.

Mentor/Mentee Committee

The Mentor/Mentee Lead oversees the planning and undertaking of the Mentor/Mentee Program, which is essential in allowing the Berkeley international community to build close-knit connections and help newer students adapt to life and academics at Berkeley. The committee will pair mentees and mentors in the beginning of the semester. It will also organize fun events to further encourage bonding between mentors and mentees.






Historian Committee

The Historian Committee is responsible for the photographic and videographic documentation of professional and social events. Through visual content, it aims to capture ISAB’s true spirit: the club’s formal aspects, the friendships, the multicultural community to which we are all proud to be part of.





Tech Committee

The Technology Committee is responsible for ISAB’s online presence. This includes the ISAB website and the creative & promotional media posted online. As a committee we work on improving and maintaining the website and building tools to aid in reaching out to alumni and to improve the communication within the club.







External Committee

The External Lead focuses on the events that ISAB holds throughout the term. This committee organizes the external ISAB activities that are not limited to the Core or General members of ISAB. Such events include main campus events and fundraisers in order to connect the members of ISAB with the external school community.
