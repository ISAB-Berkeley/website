IT’S BACK: THE ISAB Mentor/mentee program!

Purpose
IT’S BACK: The ISAB mentorship program is here to support you as you grow both professionally and personally! We will pair you with a mentor/mentee who will help you realize both professional and personal development goals, as well as build social connections within the international student community at Berkeley.

Sign Up!
For Fall 2021: You can access the sign up form here! Please try to apply by 10/29/2021, as that is our first review date for pairings.
