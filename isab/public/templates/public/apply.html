T here are two main ISAB membership options: core membership or general membership. Browse our website before choosing which one to apply to. Core members constitute a small group (less than 30) and are a fundamental part of ISAB. They run the events and are responsible of making the club tick. General members are international (and non-international) students that want to have a multicultural experience during their time at Berkeley. They attend events and make friends!
